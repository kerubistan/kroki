weak

fun <T> weak(mode: LazyThreadSafetyMode = LazyThreadSafetyMode.SYNCHRONIZED, initializer: () -> T): ReferenceDelegate<T>

Delegate to weak references.

a weak delegate is not only evaluated on demand (lazy) but also since it is tracked with a weak reference, may loose its value and therefore may be recalculated again on demand.
